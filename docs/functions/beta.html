beta( z, w )

The beta function of z and w in Math. Defined by

\[ \operatorname{B} (z,w) = \int_0^1 dt \; t^{z-1} (1-t)^{w-1} \]

Relation to gamma:

\[ \operatorname{B} (z,w) = \frac{ \Gamma(z) \Gamma(w) }{ \Gamma(z+w) } \]
